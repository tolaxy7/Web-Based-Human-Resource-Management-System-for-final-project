HRMS Help

Admin Help

The admin is responsible for managing the overall HRMS system. Here are some key tasks they handle:

  • Create and manage user accounts for HR recorders and employees.
  • Grant appropriate access levels and permissions to different users.
  • Oversee the system's configuration, security, and maintenance.
  • Handle backups, updates, and ensure the system is running smoothly.

HR Recorder Help

HR recorders are responsible for managing employee data within the HRMS system. Here are some key tasks they handle:

  • Add new employees to the system and update their information.
  • Maintain employee attendance records, leave requests, and approvals.
  • Generate reports related to employee data, such as attendance, performance, or payroll.

Employee Help

Employees interact with the HRMS system to access and update their personal information. Here are some key tasks they handle:

  • View and update their contact details, emergency contacts, and other relevant information.
  • Submit leave requests, view attendance records, and access company policies and documents.
  • View announcements, company news, and upcoming events.
  • Access self-service features like updating bank account details or submitting expense claims.
