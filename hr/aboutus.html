Company History and Background

DURAME CAMPAS (DC) is one of the fourth-generation public higher education institutions in Ethiopia, located in Durame town, 306 km away from the capital of Addis Ababa. They established the university on September 24, 2007 E.C. The teaching and learning process has commenced with its first batch of 299 (180 males and 119 females) students under four facilities and four departments. Over the last few decades, great and diverse development has been made in terms of quality education delivery, program variety, campus growth, and multi-disciplinary research activities. The university has also made exceptional efforts in substantial rehabilitation and extension of work to produce an intellectually exciting and supportive environment for teaching, learning, and research initiatives.

Mision

To deliver proficient Graduates equipped with knowledge, skill and attitude by providing relevant and quality education; produce new knowledge and technology by conducting problem solving research; transform community livelihoods by strengthening community-industry partnership, technology transfer and advisory, training healthy services; enhance sustainable institutional capacity and integrated leadership.
